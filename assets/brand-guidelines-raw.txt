Elastique H1 2026 Brand Guidelines 
Internal & Vendor Reference Document 
 
Brand Foundation 
Our Mission 
To redefine lymphatic health with elegant, science-backed everyday solutions that empower 
people to live healthier, more radiant lives. 
Our Why 
We see a world where wellness isn't an afterthought—it's an art. Where taking care of your body 
feels as exquisite as it does essential. We exist to redefine what it means to live well, merging 
science with sophistication, performance with beauty. Through innovation and intention, we 
elevate everyday rituals into acts of self-care—transforming movement, recovery, and 
restoration into something truly extraordinary. Because true luxury isn't just about what you 
wear—it's about how you feel in your own skin. 
Who We Are 
Elastique is a wearable wellness technology company that invented a patented 
circulation-enhancement system disguised as luxury fashion. We make compression garments 
embedded with MicroPerle beads that stimulate blood and lymphatic flow—solving real health 
challenges while looking beautiful enough to wear anywhere. 
We exist at the intersection of science, style, and longevity. We are not activewear. We are not 
shapewear. We are not medical devices. We are passive wellness technology you put on and 
forget about while your body benefits. 
What We Do 
We create wearable circulation optimization through: 
●​ Patented MicroPerle compression technology that enhances blood and lymph flow 
●​ Luxury garments (leggings, tops, bodysuits) designed for all-day wear 
●​ Multi-purpose solutions for workout performance, recovery, travel, extended 
sitting/standing, post-surgical healing, and longevity optimization

Our products deliver tangible, measurable results: reduced swelling, faster recovery, smoother 
skin, increased energy, and better circulation—passively, while you live your life. 
Brand Promise 
Wellness that works for you, not the other way around. 
We make high-performance health effortless. No extra time. No complicated routines. No 
sacrificing style. Just get dressed and let your body optimize itself.

Brand Voice & Tone 
Voice Characteristics 
Confident, not arrogant — We know our product works. We don't need to shout about it.

Scientific, not clinical — We explain the "why" without sounding like a medical textbook. We

Aspirational, not exclusive — We inspire people to invest in themselves without making them

What We Say (Messaging Pillars) 
Core Messages 
1.​ "Wellness without the work" 
●​ Your body optimizes itself while you live your life. No extra time, tools, or 
routines required. 
2.​ "Science you can wear" 
●​ Patented technology that delivers clinical benefits through beautiful design. This 
isn't fashion with benefits—it's technology disguised as fashion.

3.​ "Made for compliance" 
●​ Effectiveness means nothing if you won't wear it. We designed Elastique to be so 
beautiful and comfortable that wearing it is effortless. 
4.​ "Passive longevity" 
●​ The best health interventions are the ones you don't have to think about. Wear 
Elastique, forget about it, benefit for life. 
5.​ "Your body is a high-performance machine" 
●​ You maintain your car. You charge your phone. Your circulation deserves the 
same attention—and now it can get it passively. 
Supporting Claims (Always Backed by Specifics) 
●​ "Clinically proven to enhance circulation and lymphatic drainage" 
●​ "Reduces post-flight swelling by up to 40%" (use actual data) 
●​ "Accelerates workout recovery so you can train harder, more consistently" 
●​ "Smooths skin texture through improved lymphatic flow" 
●​ "Patented MicroPerle technology—nothing else works like this" 
 
What We Never Say 
Banned Phrases & Approaches 
❌ "Self-care Sunday" / "Treat yourself" — We're not indulgence, we're infrastructure for high 
performance. 
❌ "Cute" / "Adorable" / "Fun" — We are sophisticated, scientific, premium. Never diminutive. 
❌ "Detox" / "Cleanse" / "Flush toxins" — We enhance lymphatic drainage. We don't traffic in 
pseudoscience. 
❌ "Just like Spanx but healthy!" — We are not shapewear. We are medical-grade circulation 
technology. 
❌ "Perfect for busy moms!" — We don't condescend. We don't gender-limit. We speak to 
ambitious, health-conscious humans. 
❌ Overuse of "journey" / "transformation" / "empowerment" — These words have been drained 
of meaning. Be specific instead. 
❌ "Miracle" / "Magic" / "Secret" — Our results come from patented technology and clinical 
validation, not mysticism. 
❌ "Medical-grade compression that's actually stylish" — This positions us as medical first. 
We're luxury wellness first, with clinical benefits.

●​ Results they can see and feel (not placebo, not "vibes")

Brand Metrics & Success Indicators

Legal & Compliance 
Medical Claims 
●​ Always state "clinically proven" only when backed by our studies 
●​ Specify exact benefits tested (e.g., "reduces swelling," "enhances circulation") 
●​ Never diagnose or claim to treat medical conditions 
●​ Use "supports" and "enhances" rather than "cures" or "treats" 
●​ Consult medical/legal team before making new health claims 
Intellectual Property 
●​ Always reference "patented MicroPerle technology" 
●​ Protect brand language and positioning from competitor copying 
●​ Ensure collaborators use approved language about our technology

Quick Reference: Voice Checklist 
Before publishing ANY content, ask: 
✅ Is it scientific but accessible? (Not dumbed down, not jargon-heavy)  
✅ Is it confident but not arrogant? (We know our value, we don't need to oversell)  
✅ Is it specific? (No vague wellness claims, actual benefits)  
✅ Does it respect our customer's intelligence? (No condescension, no hand-holding)  
✅ Would this appeal to someone who owns an Oura ring? (Sophisticated wellness consumer)  
✅ Does it position us as technology, not fashion? (Science first, style second)  
✅ Is it free of banned phrases? (No "detox," "self-care Sunday," "miracle," etc.)
