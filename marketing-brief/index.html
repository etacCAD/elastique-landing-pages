INTERNAL REVIEW • FOR CEO

Meta Audience Strategy Brief

Strategic recommendations for ad creative and landing page alignment based on our 17 active Meta audience segments. Adhering strictly to Elastique brand guidelines and compliance constraints.

Methodology & Skills Applied

Agent Execution Summary

Approach: To generate this brief, we analyzed the provided Meta audience CSV containing 17 active ad sets. For each segment, we determined the primary psychological intent and mapped it to the highest-converting destination page framework. We then developed full-funnel ad creative (TOF, MOF, BOF) tailored to bridge the gap between the specific audience's pain points and Elastique's core value propositions.

Skills Utilized:

  • elastique-brand-voice: Enforced the "confident, scientific, aspirational" tone, applied core messaging pillars like "Zero-Routine Wellness", and strictly adhered to the banned phrases list (e.g., mitigating direct weight loss claims).
  • elastique-landing-page: Guided the strategic selection and content positioning for the destination pages (Everyday Wellness, Recovery, Longevity, Travel) relevant to each audience's psychological state.
  • elastique-ad-creative: Provided the structural framework for the full-funnel copywriting, optimizing hooks and body copy specifically for Meta's placement requirements.
  • elastique-campaign-brief: Informed the highly readable, organized HTML structure of this document to ensure it is immediately actionable for the marketing and leadership teams.

Glossary of Terms

Acronyms & Definitions
  • TOF (Top of Funnel): Creative intended for cold audiences to drive initial brand and problem awareness.
  • MOF (Middle of Funnel): Creative intended for warm audiences in the consideration phase, focusing on education, product specifics, and social proof.
  • BOF (Bottom of Funnel): Creative intended for hot audiences to drive direct conversion, focusing on strong calls-to-action, offers, and risk reversal.
  • ASC (Advantage+ Shopping Campaign): Meta's automated, machine-learning driven campaign type designed to dynamically test creative across broad audiences.
  • PDP (Product Detail Page): The specific ecommerce page on the website where a user can view details and purchase a single product.
  • DVT (Deep Vein Thrombosis): A condition where blood clots form in deep veins, often a risk highlighted for frequent travelers.
  • IRB (Institutional Review Board): An administrative body established to protect the rights and welfare of human research subjects, reflecting Elastique's clinical validation.
